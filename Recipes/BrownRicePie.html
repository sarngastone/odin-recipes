Brown Rice Pie

Serves 6 people, 40 min preparation time, 30 min cooking time

Description

A simple cheesy pie suitable for a comfortable family dinner. Best served with ketchup and hommous.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 packets brown rice
  • 2 cups cheese
  • 1 packet puff pastry
  • 1 silverbeet
  • 1 jar pasta sauce

Steps

  1. First, cook the brown rice according to the instructions on the packet.

  2. After the rice is cooked, spread it evenly in and alfoil tray.

  3. Spread the sauce on the top then cover with silverbeet leaves.

  4. Cover with cheese and pastry, then bake in the oven at ... for ... .

  5. Take out and serve!
