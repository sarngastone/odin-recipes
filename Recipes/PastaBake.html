Pasta Bake

Serves 4-6 people, 10 min preparation time, 30 min cooking time

Description

An easy cheesy classic for the whole family to enjoy, this recipe can be enhanced with lemon and hoummous or just enjoyed with a choice selection of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 500g pasta
  • 400g can of tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 5-10 basil leaves
  • 3 cups grated cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta as per instructions on the packet.

  2. While pasta is cooking put tomatoes, salt and basil in the blender and blend until smooth.

  3. When pasta is finished, strain it then pour into aluminium foil tray and cover with the sauce you made earlier.

  4. Cover with grated cheese and then put in oven at 180 degrees celcius for 30 min.

  5. Take out of the oven.

  6. Serve!
